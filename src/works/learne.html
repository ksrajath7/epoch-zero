LearnE

LearnE is an online learning app personalized for school students. We are on the mission to make online study experience easy and smooth for students. In this app the mentors can upload study notes, mock exams and question papers which can be downloaded by the students. The mentors have an option to upload video lectures which can also be downloaded to the app. We are providing a admin panel also for the mentors in which they can manage and monitor the progress of each student. LearnE also has a real-time doubt clearing feature through forum which connects the entire users of the app as well as mentors. In fact, LearnE is one of the best doubt-solving app. Be it Chemistry, Maths, Science, Physics, or any subject, experts are available 24x7!
