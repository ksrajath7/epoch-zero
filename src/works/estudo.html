Estudo

The Learning App

Estudo is an e-learning platform for competitive exams and skill based courses. Team Estudo is currently focusing on competitive exams and Commerce subjects to begin with. Our aim is to spread focus to a wide variety of courses and streams enabling students to make learning more easier and interesting. Our video and textual study materials help students in understanding the concept with the help of interesting visual aids and complex visual graphics. This makes studying easy and less complicated.
